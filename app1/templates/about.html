How to plan a trip

Know Your Limits

Understand your physical capabilities and camping experience level before planning your trip. Choose a suitable location and duration that matches your skill level.

Prepare - Gear Up!

Pack essential camping gear including tent, sleeping bags, cooking equipment, and appropriate clothing. Don't forget safety items and first-aid supplies.

Plan Your Trip

Research your camping destination, check weather forecasts, and make necessary reservations. Create a detailed itinerary and share it with someone you trust.

In Case of Emergency

Prepare for unexpected situations by bringing emergency supplies, knowing basic first aid, and having important contact numbers readily available.
